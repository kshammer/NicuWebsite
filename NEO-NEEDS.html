    Necrotizing Enterocolitis Early Warning System (Neo NEEDS)

    Behavior

    Appropriate
    Drowsy: Decreased Activity but Responds Appropriately
    Lethargic: Decreased Activity with Minimal Response to Stimulation

    Cardiovascular

    Capillary Refill <2 Sec, HR 110-160 and MAPs Stable
    Capillary Refill <5 Sec
    HR > 160 for 1 Hr
    Mean Arterial Pressure(MAP)<10% FromPrior 24 Hrs
    Capillary Refill >5 Sec
    HR > 160 for 2 Hrs
    Episode HR <100
    MAP Unstable Requiring Pressors

    Respiratory

    No Apnea/Desat Episodes. No Change in Current Settings or RR
    Apnea/Desat Self-Limited
    RR >60 for 1Hr
    Using Accessory Muscles
    Increase Periodic Breathing
    Increase Fi02 >10% FromPrior 24 Hrs
    Apnea/Desat Requiring Stimulation/Baging
    Increase RR (ie >=80) with or without Retractions
    Increase in Respiratory Support(ie Increasing Vent Pressures or Method of Ventilation)

    Abdominal

    Soft, Non-Distended, Non-Tender, Normoactive Bowel Sounds
    Slightly Distended Abdomen (1 cm FromBaseline) <5 Sec
    Hypoactive Bowel Sounds for 2 min
    Distended Abdomen (>3 cm FromBaseline)
    No Bowel Sounds for 5 min
    Guarding/Tender
    Hemoccult Positive Stools

    Feeds

    NOT Feeding
    Tolerating Bolus, No Residuals, No Emesis
    Emesis/Residuals >10% Volume, Partially Digested
    Emesis/Residuals >20% Volume
    Discolored (Bilious) Residuals/Emesis

    BW < 1000g OR GA < 28 wks

    No
    Yes

    Formula Feeds

    No
    Yes
